üìñ Complete User Guide


                    

                        
Welcome to ELOS Sentinel v3

                        

                            ELOS Sentinel v3 is an AI-powered Ethereum vanity address generator that runs entirely in your browser. 
                            It uses advanced Transformers.js AI models to intelligently optimize your search and provide smart recommendations.
                        

                    


                    

                        
üöÄ Quick Start Guide

                        
                        
Step 1: Understanding Vanity Addresses

                        

                            A vanity address is an Ethereum address that contains a specific pattern you choose. For example:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • 0x000000abc... - starts with zeros
    • 
                            
  • 0x...cafe123... - contains "cafe123"
    • 
                            
  • 0x...deadbeef - ends with "deadbeef"
    • 
                        


                        
Step 2: Using AI Pattern Analysis

                        

                            Pro Tip: Use natural language to describe what you want! The AI will understand and suggest optimal patterns.
                        

                        
Examples of what you can say:

                        
    
                            
  • "I want an address starting with my name Alice"
    • 
                            
  • "Find me an address with lucky number 777"
    • 
                            
  • "Generate address containing the word cafe"
    • 
                            
  • "I need an address ending with 1234"
    • 
                        


                        
Step 3: Configure Your Search

                        

                            Target Pattern: The exact characters you want to find (e.g., "abc123", "deadbeef")

                            Search Mode:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • Contains: Pattern can appear anywhere in the address
    • 
                            
  • Starts With: Pattern must be at the beginning (after 0x)
    • 
                            
  • Ends With: Pattern must be at the end of the address
    • 
                        


                        
Step 4: Understanding Performance Settings

                        

                            Speed (ops/pulse): How many addresses to check per cycle (higher = faster but more CPU intensive)

                            Pulse Interval: Time between cycles in milliseconds (lower = faster but more CPU intensive)
                        

                        

                            AI Optimization: The AI will automatically suggest optimal settings based on your pattern complexity!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions


                        
Q: How long will it take to find my pattern?

                        

                            It depends on pattern length and complexity. The AI provides estimates:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • 3-4 characters: Seconds to minutes
    • 
                            
  • 5-6 characters: Minutes to hours
    • 
                            
  • 7+ characters: Hours to days (or longer)
    • 
                        


                        
Q: Is this safe? Does my private key leave my browser?

                        

                            Yes, it's completely safe! Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. 
                            The AI model (Transformers.js) also runs entirely in your browser.
                        


                        
Q: Why is my search slow?

                        

                            Several factors affect speed:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • Device CPU power
    • 
                            
  • Pattern complexity
    • 
                            
  • Performance settings (Speed and Pulse Interval)
    • 
                            
  • Browser and other running programs
    • 
                        

                        
Use the AI optimization feature to automatically adjust for your device!


                        
Q: Can I use special characters in patterns?

                        

                            No, Ethereum addresses only use hexadecimal characters: 0-9 and a-f. 
                            The AI will automatically convert your input to valid hex characters.
                        


                        
Q: What's the difference between pattern difficulty levels?

                        
    
                            
  • Easy: 1-3 characters, found in seconds
    • 
                            
  • Medium: 4-5 characters, found in minutes
    • 
                            
  • Hard: 6-7 characters, may take hours
    • 
                            
  • Very Hard: 8+ characters, may take days or weeks
    • 
                        

                    


                    

                        
üîß Troubleshooting


                        
Problem: Search is not starting

                        
Solution: Ensure AI has finished loading (check top bar status). Refresh the page if needed.


                        
Problem: Browser is freezing

                        
Solution: Reduce the Speed (ops/pulse) setting. Increase Pulse Interval. Close other browser tabs.


                        
Problem: No matches found after long time

                        
Solution: Your pattern might be too complex. Use AI analysis to check difficulty. Consider a shorter or simpler pattern.


                        
Problem: AI recommendations seem incorrect

                        
Solution: The AI learns from patterns. Try describing your pattern differently or manually adjust settings.

Understanding Search Probability

  • 1 character: 1 in 16 chance
    • 
                            
  • 2 characters: 1 in 256 chance
    • 
                            
  • 3 characters: 1 in 4,096 chance
    • 
                            
  • 4 characters: 1 in 65,536 chance
    • 
                            
  • 5 characters: 1 in 1,048,576 chance
    • 
                        


                        
Optimizing for Your Hardware

                        

                            Different devices have different optimal settings:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • High-end Desktop: Speed 500K-1M, Pulse 100-200ms
    • 
                            
  • Average Laptop: Speed 200K-300K, Pulse 250-500ms
    • 
                            
  • Mobile Device: Speed 50K-100K, Pulse 500-1000ms

Security Considerations

                        

                            While vanity addresses are fun and memorable, consider:
                        

                        
    
                            
  • Don't use patterns that could identify you personally
    • 
                            
  • Longer patterns don't necessarily mean more secure
    • 
                            
  • Always verify the full address, not just the pattern

  • Use the same security practices as any crypto address
